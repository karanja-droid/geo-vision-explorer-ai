### **Q: What is GeoMiner?**
**A:** GeoMiner is an advanced AI-powered geological exploration and mining intelligence platform that helps geologists, mining companies, and exploration teams make data-driven decisions. It combines satellite imagery, geological data, drilling information, and machine learning to identify mineral prospects and optimize exploration strategies.

### **Q: How do I create an account?**
**A:** 
1. Visit [geo-miner.com](https://geo-miner.com)
2. Click "Sign Up" in the top right corner

4. Fill in your details and verify your email
5. Complete the onboarding process to set up your first project

### **Q: What subscription plans are available?**

- **Starter Team** ($19.99/month): For small exploration teams (up to 5 users)

### **Q: Is there a free trial?**

## 🤖 **AI & Machine Learning**

### **Q: How does the AI prospectivity analysis work?**
**A:** Our AI system:
1. **Analyzes your data**: Geological, geochemical, geophysical, and remote sensing data
2. **Identifies patterns**: Uses machine learning to find relationships between known deposits and geological features
3. **Generates targets**: Creates prospectivity maps showing areas with high mineral potential
4. **Provides confidence scores**: Each target comes with a confidence level (Low, Medium, High, Very High)
5. **Explains results**: Shows which features contributed most to each prediction

### **Q: What machine learning models do you use?**
**A:** We support multiple algorithms:
- **Random Forest**: Great for most geological datasets
- **Gradient Boosting**: Excellent for complex feature relationships
- **Neural Networks**: Best for large, complex datasets
- **Support Vector Machines**: Good for high-dimensional data
- **Logistic Regression**: Simple and interpretable results

### **Q: How accurate are the AI predictions?**
**A:** Accuracy varies by dataset and region, but typically:
- **Training accuracy**: 85-95% on historical data
- **Cross-validation**: 80-90% on unseen data
- **Real-world validation**: 70-85% success rate in field verification
- **Continuous improvement**: Models get better as you add more data

### **Q: Can I train my own models?**
**A:** Yes! With Corporate and Enterprise plans:
1. Upload your training data (known deposits and barren areas)
2. Select features to include in the model
3. Choose your preferred algorithm
4. Train and validate the model
5. Apply it to new areas for prospectivity analysis

## 🔬 **Laboratory & Quality Control**

### **Q: How do I manage geochemical data?**
**A:** Our LIMS (Laboratory Information Management System) features:
1. **Upload lab results**: Support for major lab formats
2. **Quality control**: Automated QC checks for standards, blanks, and duplicates
3. **Statistical analysis**: Z-score analysis, Levey-Jennings charts
4. **Chain of custody**: Track samples from field to results
5. **Reporting**: Generate professional QC reports

### **Q: What QC checks are performed automatically?**
**A:** Our system automatically checks for:
- **Standards accuracy**: Deviation from certified values
- **Blank contamination**: Unexpected values in blank samples
- **Duplicate precision**: Consistency between duplicate analyses
- **Detection limits**: Values below detection limits
- **Outlier detection**: Statistical outliers in your data

### **Q: Can I customize QC parameters?**
**A:** Yes! You can set:
- **Tolerance levels**: Define acceptable ranges for standards
- **Control limits**: Set warning and action limits
- **Element-specific rules**: Different QC rules for different elements
- **Batch requirements**: Minimum QC sample frequencies

## 🆘 **Troubleshooting**

### **Q: Why is my data upload failing?**
**A:** Common solutions:
1. **Check file size**: Ensure files are within your plan limits
2. **Verify format**: Use supported file formats
3. **Internet connection**: Ensure stable internet connection
4. **Browser cache**: Clear browser cache and cookies
5. **Contact support**: If issues persist, contact our support team

### **Q: Why are my AI results inconsistent?**
**A:** Possible causes:
- **Insufficient training data**: Need more known deposits for training
- **Data quality issues**: Check for errors or inconsistencies in input data
- **Feature selection**: Ensure relevant geological features are included
- **Model parameters**: Try different algorithms or parameter settings
- **Regional variations**: Geological processes may vary across your study area

### **Q: How do I improve AI model accuracy?**
**A:** Best practices:
1. **Quality training data**: Use high-confidence known deposits and barren areas
2. **Balanced dataset**: Include both positive and negative examples
3. **Relevant features**: Select geological features relevant to your commodity
4. **Cross-validation**: Use proper validation techniques
5. **Iterative improvement**: Continuously add new data and retrain models
