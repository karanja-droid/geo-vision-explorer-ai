# Product Overview

GeoVision AI Miner is an advanced geospatial mining intelligence platform designed for geological exploration and mining operations. The platform combines AI-driven mineral detection, satellite imagery analysis, and collaborative exploration tools to empower geologists and mining professionals.

## Core Features

- **AI-Powered Mineral Analysis**: Machine learning models for mineral prediction and classification with confidence scoring
- **Interactive 3D Mapping**: Mapbox GL JS integration with satellite imagery overlay and PostGIS spatial data support
- **Project & Site Management**: Comprehensive exploration project lifecycle management with geological site tracking
- **Real-Time Collaboration**: Live team collaboration with presence tracking and messaging
- **Advanced Analytics**: Metrics dashboards, usage analytics, and performance monitoring
- **Enterprise Security**: Role-based access control, MFA, session monitoring, and comprehensive audit trails

## User Roles

The platform supports 7 specialized mining roles with granular permissions:
- **Administrator**: Full system access and user management
- **Geologist**: Default role for project and site management
- **Geophysicist**: Advanced geophysical data analysis
- **Drilling Manager**: Drilling operations and core sample management
- **QA/QC Specialist**: Quality assurance and data validation
- **Environmental Officer**: Environmental compliance and sustainability
- **Executive**: High-level reporting and strategic decision-making
